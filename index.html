Rules

The Pig Game is a fun dice game where the objective is to reach 100 points before your opponent. On each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as they want. Each result gets added to their round score. If a player rolls a 1, they lose their current score, and it becomes the next player's turn. The player can also choose to hold, which means that their current score gets added to their total score, and it becomes the next player's turn. The first player to reach 100 points on total score wins the game.
